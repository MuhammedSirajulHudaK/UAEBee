The solution is developed with the support of Fujairah Research Center for providing insights and recommendation for local emirate’s beekeepers
The system allows beekeepers to get closer to their bees, without disturbing them. They can access the information at anytime from their internet connected devices and foresee which action they should take next
UAEBee provides up to date information on status of colony and its surroundings using cutting edge technologies. It also provides relevant research findings, weather information, market fluctuation and apiculture news
